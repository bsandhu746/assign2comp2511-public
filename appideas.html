Simple App Ideas You Can Build Now

No pressure, just practice and learning.

Pick One and Try It

  • Calender
  • Caffeine Tracker
  • Focus Timer with Study Log

Calender

A simple webpage where you track your upcoming assignments, tests, or project deadlines. You can use HTML to organize the tasks, and add CSS for a cleaner layout. It's a personal version of a online calender, except you're proud of building it.

Caffeine Tracker

During exam season, it's really easy to lose track of how many coffees or energy drinks you had. This app lets you log each drink and gives an alert if you go over your own limit. You'll get to practice forms, input validation, and basic logic, and maybe give your heart a rest too!

Focus Timer with Study Log

Create your own Pomodoro-style timer to stay focused during study sessions. Add a simple log that tracks how long you studied on a given day. It's a great way to practice timers, and you'll be able to use it while you build it! Feel free to add custom break reminders.
