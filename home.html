Welcome to Student App Ideas

A space for CIS students to explore, create, and grow.

If you're in BCIS and wondering what kind of apps you can build with the skills you've been learning, we're here to provide ideas!

This site was created by a student, for students. I know what it's like to have theory heavy classes and wanting to acctually do something with what we learn. That's why I put together a few practical app ideas that don't require much advanced coding, just motivation.

Whether you're a first year or finishing up your degree, taking on small projects can boost your confidence, help you land internships, and remind you why you chose BCIS in the first place.

By starting with these small prjects, I hope to encourage students at taking that leap to scale and make larger projects the more they learn!
